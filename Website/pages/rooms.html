Room Selection

Whether you are traveling alone or with your whole family, this hotel has a room that will accommodate your needs. For those seeking simplicity and comfort, there is a range of standard rooms to choose from with either a queen bed or a king bed. If you are traveling with more than two people, this Hotel has several options that will fit your needs, including pullout mattresses and bunk beds. If you would like something extra to fully relax and unwind, choose a jacuzzi, whirlpool, or fireplace room option. With so much to offer, this hotel truly has something for every traveler's budget. All rooms come standard with free WiFi, LCD television with premium channels, a refrigerator, a microwave, a coffee/tea maker, air conditioning, daily housekeeping, and more.
