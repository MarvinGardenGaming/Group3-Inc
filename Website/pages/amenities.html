Amenities & Services

This hotel goes beyond providing simple comfort and convenience to ensure that their guests have everything that they need for a truly enjoyable and refreshing visit. For those who like to stay active while traveling there is a convenient fitness area as well as an indoor pool. In addition, the daily housekeeping service, business center, laundry facilities, vending machines, 24-Hour Front Desk, and ATM on site keep things running smoothly. In order to continue to provide clean and orderly accommodations, pets are not allowed at the hotel. To help guests wake up and get the day started on the right foot, complimentary coffee is available, as well as a complimentary breakfast.

Conference Center

Thinking of planning your wedding or corporate event here? Look no further than this hotels conference center. With a capacity of up to 60 people, this space can host your special event with the convenience of having the hotel on-site. Inquire about our discounted rates for wedding parties and construction companies.

Fitness Center

If you don’t have the time to check out some of the wonderful active attractions in the surrounding area, this hotel makes it easy to stay in shape and get some exercise while traveling with our convenient fitness center. Whether it is a quick morning workout before an important meeting or a longer and more intense session at the end of a long day, the fitness center has you covered.

Indoor Heated Pool

Whether you are visiting during the peak of summer or the dead of winter, our heated indoor pool facilities are open year-round here at the this hotel. Come relax poolside to get caught up on your favorite book series, take a dip to refresh yourself after a long day, or maybe get a few laps in to stay active during your travel – whatever the reason, maximize your stay with us at the pool.

Free Wi-Fi

This hotel knows the importance of staying connected while on the road. Complimentary Wi-Fi is provided throughout the facilities so that you can send that important email, keep up to date with family and friends, and watch your favorite shows. In addition, wired internet access is available as well if you prefer. Hotel M is dedicated to providing fast and stable internet connection for our guests to rely on.
